About Us

Who We Are

Odini Gogo represents clients in a wide range of matters, with an emphasis on providing compassionate, knowledgeable and efficient representation to individuals and businesses. After years of practicing law at one of Atlanta’s oldest and largest law firms and as corporate counsel for a Fortune 100 corporation, Odini decided to translate his experiences into a more personalized, one‐on‐one service for his clients.

Today, Odini is proud to co-found Gogo & Moore, a firm committed to providing clients with smart, experienced and cost-effective legal representation. He offers superior counsel and proven experience to a variety of transactional and litigation matters.

Odini received his bachelor’s degree from Emory University and a law degree from Emory University School of Law. Odini is an avid reader, runner, and traveler.

Josh Moore has done a little of everything in his nearly-fifteen years practicing law. He started his practice as corporate counsel for one of the largest corporations in the Southeast, and later practiced as a litigator at one of Atlanta’s largest law firms. In his career, he has successfully engineered multi-million dollar deals, and successfully litigated cases in federal court and in Georgia trial and appellate courts. What he enjoys most about practicing law is helping people—whether it is to start a business, fight for their rights, or plan for the future.

Josh received his bachelor’s degree from the University of Georgia, and a law degree from the University of Georgia School of Law. Josh enjoys world travel, college football, and live music.
